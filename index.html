Features

Everything your business needs

One tool covering all M-Pesa platforms. No accountant required.

01

All M-Pesa Platforms

Send Money, Lipa na M-Pesa Till, Paybill, Pochi la Biashara, and Agent transactions — detected and categorized automatically.

02

Visual Dashboard

Daily income trends, expense categories, balance over time, and top contacts — rendered as clean, readable charts.

03

Profit & Loss Summary

Net profit calculated automatically after separating income, expenses, and agent transfers. Know your numbers in seconds.

04

KRA & Bank Export

One-click PDF export ready for iTax filing, loan applications, or your accountant. Clean, professional, and structured.

05

Landlord Rent Roll

Paybill rent collectors get an instant roll showing paid, partial, and outstanding — per tenant account number.

06

100% Private

All processing happens in your browser. Your statement is never uploaded, stored, or transmitted to any server.

How It Works

Three steps. Thirty seconds.

No learning curve. No configuration. Just upload and see.

01

Download Your Statement

Get your M-Pesa statement from the MySafaricom app or by dialing *334#. Save it as CSV or PDF to your device.

02

Upload to PesaLens

Drag and drop your file into PesaLens. No login, no signup, no account required. Works on any browser or device.

03

Read Your Insights

Instantly see total income, expenses, net profit, charts, top contacts, and download your clean financial report.
